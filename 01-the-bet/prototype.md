## The Bet in One Sentence

Menza AI gives founders and business leaders an always-on AI analyst that proactively monitors their business, explains what is happening, and recommends actions, eliminating the need for manual reporting and reactive decision-making.

---

## Kill Criteria

We would stop pursuing this direction if:

* Users continue relying on traditional dashboards and reports instead of AI-generated insights.
* AI recommendations are not trusted or acted upon by decision-makers.
* Users do not achieve measurable time savings compared to existing BI workflows.
* Large platforms such as Shopify, Salesforce, HubSpot, or Microsoft provide equivalent proactive business intelligence natively.
* Retention remains low after initial onboarding, indicating the product is perceived as a novelty rather than a critical business tool.
* Customers are unwilling to connect business-critical systems due to trust, security, or data privacy concerns.
